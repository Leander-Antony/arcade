# 🕹️ Our Neon Arcade

Okay, so I built this entire retro arcade platform just so we could play against each other. 

I didn't want it to feel like just another boring, clean website. I wanted it to feel like a massive, heavy, physical arcade cabinet from the 80s that we just plugged in. 

Here is what you're getting yourself into:

---

## 🎨 The Vibe & Feel

I went completely overboard trying to make this feel like a real, physical machine:
* **The Screen**: You'll notice the whole screen has this curved glass shadow and a slow scanline rolling down it, just like a heavy old CRT television. 
* **The Physicality**: I literally programmed a physics engine so that when you click "Insert Coin", a massive 8-bit coin drops from the sky and bounces into the slot.
* **The Screen Shake**: This is the best part. When you crash your bike or hit a trap, the *entire browser window violently shakes*. I wanted you to actually feel it when you lose.
* **The Sound**: There are no MP3 files in this. I built a custom audio engine that generates raw synthesizer waves in real-time. So when you hear a beep or a crash, it's actual math making that sound just like the original 1980s arcade chips.

---

## 🎮 The Games

I built 5 different games for us to play. Prepare to lose.

### 1. 🏍️ Tron Lightcycles
This is a high-speed survival duel. We both drive these neon lightcycles that leave a solid wall behind them. The goal is to box me in and force me to crash into a wall (or your trail). If you crash, the whole screen shakes. First to 3 wins!

### 2. 🧀 Mouse Duel
A chaotic, reflex-based arena where items pop up all over the screen. 
You have to hover your glowing cursor over the items before I steal them. 
* **Stars** get you 1 point.
* **Cakes** get you 5 points.
* But if you accidentally grab a **Trap** or a **Bomb**, you lose points and the screen rattles. 

### 3. 🃏 Memory Flip
You know how to play Memory—take turns flipping cards to find emoji pairs. 
But I added a twist: If you find a match, you steal a point and you *keep your turn*. 
I also added a **5x5 Joker Mode**. It has 12 normal pairs and exactly one Joker card. If you happen to click the Joker, you instantly get a free point and keep going. 

### 4. 🔴 Button Chaos
A massive grid of 64 buttons. We just violently click as fast as humanly possible to claim the buttons for our own color. Every now and then a massive Power-Up spawns that claims a huge 3x3 area. It is pure chaos.

### 5. 🧩 Puzzle Co-Op 
For when you get tired of me beating you in the competitive games. We can upload any picture we want, and it instantly shatters into a jigsaw puzzle across both of our screens. We can actually see each other's cursors moving around in real-time as we work together to rebuild it.

---

So... click the **Insert Coin** button, wait for the drop, and let's see what you've got. 🪙
